BOROUGHBRIDGE & DISTRICT ANGLING CLUB understands that its activities impact upon the environment and is committed to the following environmental policy:

Environmental Policy

Boroughbridge & District Angling Club will:
  • Minimise the impacts of its usage on the natural and social environment through effective environmental management.
  • Integrate environmental management into our club and its members.
  • Review its environmental performance with members to establish continual improvement.
  • Increase the environmental awareness of all its members.
  • Establish systems for the prevention of pollution at all its fishing sites.
  • Comply with all the environmental legislation, regulations and other requirements that are relevant to its activities.
  • Report on its environmental performance to all interested parties.
