The Roecliffe Rose Bowl Sponsored by Garry Tilburn

The Roecliffe Rose Bowl Angling Contest, organised by the Boroughbridge and District Angling Club, is an exciting event open to all junior members. This angling match provides young anglers with an opportunity to showcase their skills and compete for enticing prizes.

The contest takes place in the picturesque setting of Roecliffe pond, a charming village located in North Yorkshire, England. Known for its tranquil waterways and abundant fish population, Roecliffe offers the perfect backdrop for a thrilling angling competition.

Junior members of the Boroughbridge and District Angling Club, regardless of their level of experience, are encouraged to participate in the contest. Whether they are seasoned anglers or new to the sport, this event provides a platform for young enthusiasts to engage in friendly competition and enhance their angling abilities.

The Boroughbridge and District Angling Club places great emphasis on the conservation of fish and the environment. Therefore, the contest follows strict guidelines and regulations to ensure the ethical treatment of fish and the preservation of their habitats. Anglers are expected to adhere to these guidelines to maintain the sustainability of the local fishing resources.

This fabulous series is sponsored by Garry Tilburn, the club's membership secretary and treasurer, who has been a dedicated member for over 50 years. His support and involvement in the event is truly commendable.


Inter Shool Fishing Tournament Sponsored by Ian's Floats

Great news! Entry is now open for the highly anticipated inter-school fishing tournament at Woodlands Lakes in Thirsk. This is an excellent opportunity for junior anglers from schools in the North Yorkshire region to showcase their fishing skills and compete against other talented anglers.

To enter your school's team in the tournament, please contact the Boroughbridge and District Angling Club. They will provide you with all the necessary information and guide you through the registration process. Don't miss out on this fantastic chance to be part of an exciting fishing event and represent your school with pride.

Whether your school has experienced anglers or students who are new to the sport, this tournament welcomes everyone to participate and enjoy a day of fishing in the beautiful surroundings of Woodlands Lakes. With the support and guidance of the organising committee and sponsors, this event promises to be a memorable and fulfilling experience for all involved.

Sponsored by Woodlands Fishery in Thirsk and Middy Tackle, the inter-school fishing tournament organised by the Boroughbridge and District Angling Club promises to be an unforgettable event for junior anglers in the North Yorkshire region. With the generous support of these esteemed sponsors, the tournament aims to provide young anglers with a unique and rewarding experience.

After an exciting day of fishing, all participants and their families can look forward to an awards ceremony, where the top-performing teams will be recognised and celebrated. Prizes ranging from fishing equipment to vouchers for fishing trips will be awarded to the winners, adding an extra element of excitement and motivation to the tournament.

Junior Champion Sponsored by Richard & Shiela Potter

The Boroughbridge and District Angling Club Junior Champion is an esteemed title awarded to the angler who has accumulated the most points throughout the 2024 season. This prestigious award is sponsored by Richard and Sheila Potter, dedicated friends and supporters of the club.

Throughout the 2024 season, junior anglers participate in various events and competitions organised by the club. These events take place in a range of fishing venues, including rivers, lakes, and canals in the local area. Each angler earns points based on their performance in these competitions.

The sponsorship of the championship by Richard and Sheila Potter reflects their passion for angling and their commitment to supporting the development of young anglers. Their generous support allows the club to provide prizes and recognition for the Junior Champion, as well as other incentives to encourage the participation of junior anglers.


Aggregate weight Sponsored by Bren & Dave

One of the notable highlights of the club is the prestigious Aggregate Weight competition, which takes place throughout the 2024 angling season. This competition aims to determine the highest weight achieved by a junior angler over the course of the season. It serves as a platform to encourage young anglers to showcase their skills and passion for the sport.

The Aggregate Weight competition is made possible through the generous sponsorship of angler Dave Crawford and his partner Bren Dawson. Dave Crawford, a seasoned angler with a wealth of experience, has been an active member of the Boroughbridge & District Angling Club for many years. Recognising the importance of supporting young talent and fostering a love for angling, Dave and Bren have graciously sponsored the competition to encourage junior anglers to excel in their pursuit of the sport.


Junior & Senior Pairs Sponsored by Crackerjack BBQ

The Boroughbridge and District Angling Club is excited to announce its upcoming Junior and Senior Pairs Competition. This unique event brings together junior and senior members of the club to form teams and compete for the top prize. Sponsored by Crackerjack BBQ, the competition promises a sizzling prize for the winning team.

The event is not only about the thrill of competition but also about promoting a love for angling among the younger generation. The junior members will have the opportunity to learn from the senior anglers, gaining valuable insights and techniques that will enhance their fishing skills.
