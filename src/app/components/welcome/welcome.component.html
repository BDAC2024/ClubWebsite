Welcome to the Boroughbridge & District Angling Club. The club has a selection of waters, some being members only and some having day or guest tickets available.

Unrestricted access is available to: -
  • News
  • Club waters having day ticket access
  • Club Officers
  • Club Forms (available here)
Members get additional access to: -
  • Members-only club waters
  • Diary of events
  • Matches
    • Fixtures
    • Results
    • League standings
    • Aggregate weight standings
  • Rules
In addition to this website, the club also has a Facebook Group and a YouTube Channel (links to both available at the top of the page).
