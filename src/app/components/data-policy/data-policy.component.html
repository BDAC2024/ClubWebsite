Privacy Notice

Introduction

For the purposes of this document, Boroughbridge & District Angling Club is referred to as 'the club'.

This document outlines the club's policy with respect to your data and compliance with GDPR (General Data Protection Regulations).

Purpose of the Data Processing

Your information will be stored to enable the club to provide membership services and/or day/guest tickets to enable you to fish the club's waters.

Information Held

The club holds: -

Membership Information (people paying to be members)


Membership Spreadsheet (held by Membership Secretary)

Name, Address, Phone Number, Address, Email Address, Membership Category, Membership Status.

Memberships (held within the website database)

Name, Email Address, Membership Status. Because the website uses a third-party payment company (Stripe), credit card and other billing details are not stored or controlled by the club.

Day/Guest Ticket Holders (people paying for tickets)


Ticket Sales Stubs (collected by Membership Secretary from sales outlets)

Name.

Orders (held within the website database)

Name.

Data Sharing

Your information will not be shared with any third parties. However, online purchases are processed by Stripe who will hold payment/billing details. These details are not stored by the club.

Third Parties

Stripe is GDPR compliant. Their own Privacy Notice is available when using Stripe to pay for the club's services.

Retention Period

Your membership data will be retained whilst you remain a club member. If your membership should lapse, the information will be deleted after 6 years.

Your Rights

Your rights under the GDPR are not affected and you have the right to view your data, correct any error or ask for it to be erased. You can ask for a copy of your records at any time.

Withdrawal of Consent

You can cancel your membership at any time and request removal of information where feasible.

Automated Processing

The club does not use any automated decision making, data processing or profiling technologies.

Identity and Contact Details

Your information is managed by the membership secretary whose details are publicly available on the club's website under the 'Club Info '> Club Officers' menu option.

Your Right to Lodge a Complaint with a Supervisory Authority

If you wish to complain about the handling of your personal data; In the first instance, please raise a complaint with the membership secretary (contact details above). If you do not get a satisfactory response, you can make a complaint to the information Commissioner's office (www.ico.org.uk).

Consequence of choosing not to provide Personal Information

If you choose not to provide personal information for us to process, the club would be unable to provide membership services for you or sell you tickets to fish our waters.

Related Documents

These documents are created in Microsoft Word. They are held by the Data Protection Officer and can be provided on request from the membership secretary. The Privacy Notice (this document) is also published on the club's website.
