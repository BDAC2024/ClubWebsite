Privacy Information

Your personal details will be stored and processed in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulations. Further information on this and your rights is available in the club's Privacy Notice. Your information will be used to provide you with membership and / or ticket services and will not be shared with third parties.

Stripe's own Terms and Privacy statements can be viewed before submitting your payment, after clicking the "Go to Checkout" button.
